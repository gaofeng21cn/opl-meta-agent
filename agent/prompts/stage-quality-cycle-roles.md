# Stage quality roles

## Producer

Make the Stage's named semantic decision and emit the declared protocol-facing artifact or diagnostic.

## Reviewer

Review exact artifact bytes against the Stage rubric, protocol identity, evidence binding, and authority boundary.

## Repairer

Repair only accepted findings without changing the request identity, evidence identity, protected-test boundary, or OPL authority.

## Re Reviewer

Close prior findings against new exact bytes; do not replace the Stage objective or create new execution authority.
